Frequently asked questions

How do I rate a place?

Open the Search tab, find the café or restaurant, tap it to open its page, then tap Add rating. Choose whether you ordered a single or double espresso, then rate its size and taste and note whether you were served water.

How do I sign in?

Go to the Profile tab and sign in with Apple or Google. An account lets your ratings and favorites sync and appear again if you reinstall the app or switch devices.

Where do "My Places" and "Favorites" come from?

My Places lists everywhere you've rated. Favorites lists the places you've marked with the heart on a place's page. Both are restored automatically after you sign in.

My ratings or favorites are missing.

Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple or Google account you used before, and that you have an internet connection. Your data is tied to your account and is fetched after sign-in.

Are my photos and notes private?

Yes. Notes and photos you attach to a rating are private to your account and are never shown to other users. Photos are stored securely in the European Union.

Does it work offline?

You can browse your rated places and favorites offline. Search, community scores, and editing require an internet connection.

How do I change the language?

Go to Profile and pick a language from the dropdown, or choose Automatic to follow your device's language.

How do I delete my account and data?

Go to Profile and tap Delete account. This permanently deletes your account, ratings, favorites, notes, and photos. This cannot be undone.
